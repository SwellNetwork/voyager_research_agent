# PUMP Research Focus

## Primary Narrative: Meme Token Launchpad / Fee Revenue

PUMP.fun should be analyzed through the lens of:
- **Fee Revenue**: Token launch fees and trading fees
- **Buyback Mechanism**: Fee-funded token buybacks
- **Meme Coin Market**: Leading launchpad for Solana meme tokens
- **Viral Dynamics**: Social sentiment and trending token activity
- **Protocol Economics**: Revenue sustainability and distribution

## Section-Specific Guidance

### Executive Summary Emphasis
- Lead with fee revenue and buyback metrics
- Reference viral token activity (trending, viewers)
- Highlight protocol revenue vs peers
- Note market sentiment and meme coin cycle position

### Valuation Model Anchors

**Bull Case Triggers:**
- Fee revenue > $1M/day sustained
- Major viral token success
- Buyback rate acceleration
- Meme coin "summer" narrative

**Base Case Assumptions:**
- Moderate fee revenue
- Steady buyback rate
- Normal meme activity levels

**Bear Case Triggers:**
- Fee revenue decline > 50%
- Meme coin fatigue
- Competition from other launchpads
- Platform issues or exploits

### Risk Assessment Specifics

**PUMP-Specific Risks:**
1. **Meme Cycle Dependency**: Revenue tied to meme coin interest
2. **Platform Risk**: Smart contract vulnerabilities
3. **Competition**: Other launchpads (e.g., moonshot)
4. **Regulatory Risk**: Token launch classification
5. **Reputation Risk**: Scam tokens association
6. **Solana Dependency**: Platform chain risk

**Invalidation Levels:**
- Fundamental: Fee revenue < $100K/day sustained
- Market: Meme coin total market cap decline > 50%
- Platform: Major exploit or downtime
