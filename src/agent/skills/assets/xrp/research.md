# XRP Research Focus

## Primary Narrative: Cross-Border Remittance / Regulatory Clarity

XRP should be analyzed through the lens of:
- **Regulatory Story**: SEC case outcomes and clarity
- **Bank Partnerships**: SWIFT alternative thesis
- **ODL Adoption**: On-Demand Liquidity volume growth
- **Institutional Use Case**: Cross-border payment infrastructure
- **Legal Precedent**: Impact on broader crypto regulation
